CS-Kaspi v6 seed-listing fresh full repo

Core idea:
- Official site = model/spec/SEO reference.
- Ozon/WB = only user-provided seed listing URLs.
- No fallback search by brand/model.
- No product-page-first parsing.
- Listing parser uses Playwright scroll to collect lazy-loaded cards.
- Same sellable variants are collapsed; lowest source price wins.
- Kaspi price = source price + 30%.
- Ozon/WB ETA is copied without extra safety buffer.
- Kaspi live-send is disabled; only draft API/XML/preview files are generated.

- config/suppliers/demiand.yml
