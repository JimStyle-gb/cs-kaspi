CS-Kaspi v17 brand_all clean baseline

Production WB source:
- https://www.wildberries.ru/brands/demiand/all

Removed from production logic:
- old category seed links
- WB wide search by query "demiand"
- review/search discovery as a normal build step

Business rules preserved:
- WB brand_all is the sellable market source.
- Official Demiand site enriches specs/SEO/photos/descriptions, but is not a hard sellable filter.
- WB-only products with DEMIAND brand are kept and enriched from WB/model specs/duplicates when official match is missing.
- Exact duplicates of the same sellable variant are collapsed; lowest WB price wins.
- Different color/volume/model/Wi-Fi/bundle/accessory/count/material/type/WB variant remain separate Kaspi candidates.
- Price rule: lowest WB price + 30%, then floor to last two digits 00.
- No extra delivery buffer over WB ETA.
- Kaspi category codes are verified against data/kaspi/reference/kaspi_categories.json.
- Kaspi templates included: air fryers, blenders, accessories for small kitchen appliances, tabletop electric ovens, coffee machines.
- Photo package keeps source JPG/JPEG without needless recompression; WebP/PNG convert to JPG quality 95; no resizing.

Notes:
- Live Kaspi API sending remains disabled by design.
- Generated artifacts/images should stay in GitHub Actions artifacts, not committed to repository.
